# tmux cheat sheet

## Session management

|Action  | Command |
|--------|---------|
|Start session | `tmux new -s session-name` |
|Attach to session | `tmux a -t session-name` |

## Window management

|Action         | Command |
|---------------|---------|
|Create window  | `c`     |
|List windows   | `w`     |
|Next window    | `n`     |
|Previous window| `p`     |
|Find window    | `f`     |
|Name window    | `,`     |
|Kill window    | `&`     |

## Panes

|Action          | Command |
|Vertical split  | `|`     |
|Horizontal split| `\`     |
|Kill pane       | `x`     |
|Move left       | `C-l`   |
|Move right      | `C-h`   |
|Move up         | `C-j`   |
|Move down       | `C-k`   |
